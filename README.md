## What is NEUROVEIL?

NEUROVEIL is an intelligent cybercrime investigation platform designed to function as a Digital Cyber Investigation Officer.

Modern cybercrime investigations often involve thousands of complaints, massive transaction datasets, multiple digital identities, cryptocurrency wallets, social engineering evidence, and rapidly moving funds. Traditional investigation workflows struggle to process this volume of information efficiently.

NEUROVEIL addresses this challenge by combining:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Machine Learning
* Digital Forensics
* Graph Intelligence
* Fraud Analytics
* Human Authorization Workflows

into a single investigation ecosystem capable of assisting investigators from complaint intake to evidence generation.

The platform does not simply store complaints.

It actively investigates them.

---

## The Problem

Cybercrime investigations are becoming increasingly complex.

Investigators must analyze:

* Fraudulent bank transactions
* UPI payment trails
* Crypto transfers
* Scam websites
* Fake investment platforms
* Social media scams
* Telegram fraud groups
* Phishing campaigns
* Identity theft incidents
* Digital evidence

A single scam operation may involve:

```text
50+ Victims
20+ Bank Accounts
10+ UPI IDs
Multiple Devices
Several Wallets
Numerous Fake Identities
```

Manually connecting these relationships requires significant time and resources.

By the time evidence is connected, funds may already be moved through multiple laundering layers.

NEUROVEIL is designed to reduce this gap.

# Core Philosophy

NEUROVEIL is built around three principles:

## 1. Human-Like Investigation

The AI should investigate similarly to how an experienced cyber investigator thinks.

It should:

* Analyze evidence
* Correlate clues
* Build timelines
* Detect relationships
* Trace transactions
* Identify patterns
* Generate hypotheses
* Explain findings

---

## 2. Human Authorization

The AI never acts as a legal authority.

Instead:

```text
AI Investigates
Human Reviews
Human Approves
AI Continues
```

All legally sensitive actions remain under authorized human control.

---

## 3. Continuous Learning

Every investigation improves future investigations.

The platform learns from:

* Officer feedback
* Investigation outcomes
* False positives
* Confirmed fraud cases
* Recovery results
