Learning Corner

Understand common cyber crimes and how to avoid them.

Phishing

Fake links and emails that try to steal your passwords or personal data.

OTP Fraud

Never share OTP with anyone. Banks never ask for your OTP or PIN.

Fake Calls

Scammers may pretend to be bank or police officers to trick you.

UPI Scam

Always check the payment request before approving anything.

Safety Tips

  • Do not click unknown links.
  • Use strong passwords.
  • Never share OTP, PIN, or CVV.
  • Verify every call or message.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately.
